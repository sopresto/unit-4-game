Pick a Bandicoot

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four Crash Bandicoot characters below. By clicking on a character you will add a specific amount of points to your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each character is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the value of the characters will change.
